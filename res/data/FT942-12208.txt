Johannesburg was firmer but ended the day off earlier highs on late
profit-taking. Traders said that a stronger bullion price had encouraged
investors. The overall index added 118 to 5,358, industrials 154 to 6,411
and golds firmed 94 to 2,000. SAB rose R3.50 to R97.50, Anglos R3 to R235
and Vaal Reefs R21 to R416.
