John Brown, part of Trafalgar House of the UK, yesterday announced two big
US contracts totalling Dollars 70m (Pounds 47m), both awarded by European
chemicals companies, writes Andrew Baxter. The larger deal, worth Dollars
40m, is to design and construct a speciality chemicals plant in Theodore,
Alabama for Huls, the German chemical company. John Brown has also won a
Dollars 30m contract to provide engineering and procurement services for
Solvay Polymers' polyethylene finishing complex at Deer Park, Texas.
