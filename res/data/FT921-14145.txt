Net asset value of Group Development Investment Trust rose from 34.5p to
39.5p in the year to September 30 1991.
Net revenue amounted to Pounds 55,606, against Pounds 44,777 in the previous
year, giving earnings per 10p share of 0.23p (0.19p).
The single distribution for the year is up to 0.24p (0.2p).
