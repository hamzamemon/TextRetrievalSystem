HYUNDAI, South Korea's largest business group, is to merge a number of its
subsidiaries, reducing them from 44 to 36, AP-DJ reports.
It will merge Korea Industry Service and Hyundai Steel Tower into Hyundai
Electric; Hyundai Resources Development into Hyundai Corp; and Hyundai Robot
Industries into Hyundai Electronics. It will also sever managerial control
of Keumgang Development Industrial; Hanmoo Shopping; Hyundai Marine and Fire
Insurance; and Hyundai Aluminum Industry.
