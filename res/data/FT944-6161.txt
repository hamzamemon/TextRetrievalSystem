Tops Estates, the shop and office property company, yesterday announced a 62
per cent increase in interim pre-tax profits from Pounds 825,000 to Pounds
1.34m.
Rental income for the six months to September 30 jumped from Pounds 6.84m to
Pounds 9.02m, including income from the two shopping centres in Lancashire
acquired in January and 14 weeks rent from the Bond Street shopping centre
in Leeds.
Net interest charges increased to Pounds 6.67m (Pounds 5.16m).
Earnings per share fell to 0.8p (1.4p) after tax of Pounds 970,000 (Pounds
175,000). The interim dividend goes up to 0.64p (0.61p).
