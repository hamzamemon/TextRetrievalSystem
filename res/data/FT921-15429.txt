Bowater, the packaging, printing and industrial films group, is to sell
Interchecks, its US cheque printing business, for Dollars 50m (Pounds 27.4m)
cash, which will be used to reduce borrowings.
Interchecks, based in Seattle with a presence mainly on the west coast of
the US, is being bought by John H Harland.
In 1991 Interchecks made operating profits of Dollars 1.9m on sales of about
Dollars 72m. Its net asset value at the end of 1991 was about Dollars 24m.
