Bankers Trust, US bank, said derivatives were not responsible for the sharp
fall in first-quarter trading profits from Dollars 346m to Dollars 14m
(Pounds 9.5m) it reported on Tuesday. It said it saw a short-term slowdown
in client demand for derivatives, but no longer term reduction in the use of
the instruments. Results, Page 36; Echoes in Europe, Page 31; Editorial
