North Midland Construction, the engineering and construction company,
announced pre-tax profits almost halved from Pounds 1.02m to Pounds 555,000
for the year to December 31 1991.
Turnover was slightly ahead to Pounds 24.4m (Pounds 24m).
Earnings per share dropped from 6.5p to 3.8p and the dividend for the year
is cut to 1p (1.25p) via a proposed 0.5p (0.75p) final.
