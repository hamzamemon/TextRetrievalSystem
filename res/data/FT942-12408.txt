Undiluted net asset value per ordinary share of the Abtrust New Thai
Investment Trust improved from 133.02p to 179.08p over the 12 months ended
February 28. The fully diluted figure emerged at 165.9p against 127.52p.
After-tax revenue rose to Pounds 206,159 (Pounds 183,925), equal to earnings
per share of 1.37p (1.23p). The single dividend for the year is maintained
at 1p.
