Peter Alden has been appointed md of CAMBRIDGE CABLE, the Comcast &amp;
Singapore Telecom joint venture. British-born, he has worked on both sides
of the Atlantic, starting his career as a design engineer with Marconi; he
has been md of Videotron UK, an executive vice-president of Warner Cable in
New York, md of Westminster Cable, and joins from Chase International in
Connecticut where he was ceo of Polish Cable Television.
