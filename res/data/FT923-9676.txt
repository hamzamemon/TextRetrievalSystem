Exceptional charges of Pounds 1.19m left William Jackson &amp; Son with losses
of Pounds 1.01m in the year to April 25, against with profits of Pounds
2.98m. Turnover fell from Pounds 207.8m to Pounds 205.8m.
Losses per share for this company, which trades as a baker, meat products
manufacturer and discount stores operator, came out at 31.4p, against
earnings of 77.5p.
