Landed demand continued strong and general, report the Tea Brokers'
Association. Brighter liquoring East African teas sold readily at fully firm
to dearer rates. Mediums showed irregularity and plainer sorts were easier
with quality. Ceylons met good support at firm rates. Offshore improved
competition for most of the cif offerings with brighter Ceylon and Zimbabwe
teas a good feature. Quotations: quality 155p nom, good medium 135p, medium
105p and low medium 85p. The highest price realised this week was 171p for a
Rwanda pf. 1.
