CAMBIOR, the Canadian ore producer, earned CDollars 2m (USDollars 1.58m), or
6 cents a share, in the third quarter, down from CDollars 3.1m, or 10 cents,
last time, on revenues of CDollars 46.7m.
At nine months, profit was CDollars 11.1m, or 34 cents a share, compared
with CDollars 13.9m, or 45 cents, on revenues of CDollars 143m, up 13 per
cent.
