Suter, the industrial conglomerate, has completed the sale of the 24-acre
Lyne Hill industrial estate at Penkridge, Stafford, to Great Portland
Estates for Pounds 11m cash.
The deal is subject to a 19-year leaseback to Suter Estates at an initial
annual rent of Pounds 1.1m, rising to a minimum of Pounds 1.3m at the first
review period in five years' time.
