Pre-tax profits of South Staffordshire Water Holdings improved by 11 per
cent, from Pounds 4.6m to Pounds 5.1m, in the six months to end-September.
The outcome was achieved on turnover up by Pounds 2m to Pounds 25.9m. The
interim dividend is raised to 15p from a pro-forma 13.4p last time, payable
from earnings per share of 78.7p (73.9p pro-forma).
