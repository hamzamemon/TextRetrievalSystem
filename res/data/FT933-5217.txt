Sleepy Kids, the children's animation and merchandising group traded on the
USM, has signed four licensing agreements in the US for Budgie The Little
Helicopter merchandise.
The licenses, the company's first in the US, are with Franco Manufacturing
for a line of beach and bath towels, with Selandia Designs for children's
melamine dishes, with Nylint Corporation for plastic vehicle toys, and with
Happiness Express for small toys and room accessories.
