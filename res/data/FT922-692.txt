Net asset value per share at Electra Investment Trust declined to 285.84p at
the end of the six months to March 31, against 304.53p at September 30 1991.
Net assets of Pounds 469m (Pounds 502m) included interests in Electra
Kingsway valued at Pounds 10m.
The trust adopted a cautious stance and made few investments during the
first half. It has however raised the interim dividend to 3.3p (3.2p).
Attributable profit increased to Pounds 6.46m (Pounds 6.18m).
