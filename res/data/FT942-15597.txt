Mr Deverok Pritchard made a paper profit of some Pounds 32.1m yesterday when
he sold almost all of his 10.2 per cent stake in Takare, the healthcare
group he co-founded and from which he was ousted in a boardroom row over
management style last month.
He sold some 12.5m shares, reducing his holding to 97,456 shares. The share
price was unchanged at 258p.
