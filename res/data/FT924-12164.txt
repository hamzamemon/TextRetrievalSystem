WOOLWICH, the country's fourth-largest building society, yesterday cut its
standard variable mortgage rate to 9.3 per cent from 9.99 per cent with
immediate effect for new borrowers and at a date to be announced for
existing borrowers.
Bradford &amp; Bingley, the seventh-largest building society, on Tuesday dropped
its mortgage rate from 9.95 per cent to 9.25 per cent. The cut will take
effect on December 1 for existing borrowers.
