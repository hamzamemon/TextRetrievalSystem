EFM Income Trust, which was launched in April 1991, reported net asset value
of 39.1p per zero dividend preference share at April 30. Net asset value per
ordinary share at the same date was 47.5p.
Net revenue for the period from launch to the end of April was Pounds
840,000. The ordinary holders are to receive a proposed dividend of 1.275p
making a total of 4.875p, achieving the forecast yield of 10 per cent.
Earnings were 5.6p.
