MAY Department Stores, the St Louis-based retailer, is to invest Dollars
4.6bn over the next five years in opening 100 department stores and 1,200
Payless ShoeSource stores.
The expansion was announced at the annual meeting, when Mr David Farrell,
chairman and chief executive, said the company expected record sales and
per-share earnings in 1993. Earlier this month, the retailer reported
first-quarter net earnings of Dollars 96m, up from Dollars 81m, on net
retail sales 5.3 per cent higher at Dollars 2.32bn.
The company planned to open 13 department stores and 225 Payless ShoeSource
stores this year.
