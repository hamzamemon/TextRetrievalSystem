Radius, the USM-traded computer systems and maintenance company, incurred a
pre-tax loss of Pounds 1.17m for the 13 months ended December 31 on turnover
of Pounds 23.3m.
That compared with profits of Pounds 1.23m for the 1993 year on a turnover
of Pounds 24.01m.
The final dividend is being omitted (1.8p) leaving shareholders with 0.45p
(2.7p) for the period. Losses per share emerged at 3p (earnings 2.9p).
The shares dipped 3p to 28p.
