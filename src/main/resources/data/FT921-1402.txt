ELY CATHEDRAL has been granted Pounds 345,000 this year by English Heritage,
the ancient monuments watchdog, to help with the Pounds 1.4m restoration of
the Norman south-west transept. A further Pounds 345,000 has been promised
for 1993-94. The grant is part of a total of Pounds 5.4m for urgent repairs
and conservation work at English cathedrals over the next three years. Ely
and Salisbury are the main beneficiaries.
