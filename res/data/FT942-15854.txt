Five Oaks Investments, the property investor and developer, swung from
losses of Pounds 953,000 to profits of Pounds 655,000 pre-tax for the half
year to December 31.
Turnover of continuing operations rose to Pounds 2.33m (Pounds 1.82m).
Earnings were 0.8p (losses 2.04p) and dividends are resumed via an interim
of 0.2p.
There was an exceptional gain of Pounds 396,000.
