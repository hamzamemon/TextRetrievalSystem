Rentokil, the environmental services and property care group, has acquired a
49 per cent stake in Nippon Calmic, a joint venture company in Japan, for
Pounds 5.5m in cash. The vendor is Wellcome.
The joint venture partner is Kyoritsu Shoji, a privately-owned veterinary
pharmaceutical company. Nippon Calmic, formed in 1969, provides the Calmic
sanitizer and other healthcare services. Its pre-tax profits for the year to
May 31 1991 were Y310m (Pounds 1.36m) on turnover of Y3.15bn.
