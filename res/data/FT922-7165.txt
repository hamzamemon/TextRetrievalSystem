Apollo Metals reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 711,000 for the six months
to March 31, against Pounds 969,000. Turnover fell from Pounds 14.3m to
Pounds 12m.
Earnings per share were 3.4p (4.8p). But the interim dividend is being
maintained at 1.15p.
Gearing remained at less than 10 per cent. Interest payments dropped from
Pounds 100,000 to Pounds 58,000.
