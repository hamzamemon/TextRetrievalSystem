VISTA Chemical, the Houston-based commodity and speciality chemicals company
subject to a tender offer from RWE, the German utility, yesterday reported a
profits improvement in the second quarter of its financial year, writes
Nicki Tate.
It made Dollars 18.2m in the three months to the end of March, compared with
Dollars 16.2m last time.
The company saw sales of Dollars 191.6m in the period, compared with Dollars
184.8m in its second quarter in 1990.
