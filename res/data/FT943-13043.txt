The Jubilee underground line extension site at the corner of Redcross Way
and Southwark Street, near London Bridge station south of the Thames. The
site is one of five access points to the Pounds 1.9bn tunnel link, which is
being dug 30 metres below the surface by contractors Costain and Taylor
Woodrow.
London Underground said that overall the project was 'just about on time'.
The extension is scheduled to be completed by March 1998
