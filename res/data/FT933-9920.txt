LVMH Moet Hennessy Louis Vuitton, the French luxury products group, has
reached an agreement to acquire control of France's Defosses International
for FFr110m (Dollars 19m), AP-DJ reports from Paris.
Defosses said that while 'a large majority of shareholders' had approved the
sale, they would meet again on August 23 to finalise it and work out
details. Defosses said the accord included a restructuring of the publishing
company's bank debts.
Defosses is prominent in French business newspapers, producing the dailies
Agefi and La Tribune-Defosses.
