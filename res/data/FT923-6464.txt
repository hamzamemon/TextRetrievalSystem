THE New York Times plans to close the Gwinnett Daily News, of Gwinnett
County, Georgia, and sell the residual property to Cox Enterprises for
undisclosed terms, Reuter reports from New York.
The newspaper said it will take a Dollars 0.45 per share third-quarter
charge for the deal.
Cox publishes the Atlanta Journal and Constitution. The proposed transaction
is being reviewed by the US Justice Department.
