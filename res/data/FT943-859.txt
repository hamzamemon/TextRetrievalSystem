Banner Homes Group is raising about Pounds 2.9m net through an issue to
Throgmorton Preferred Income Trust of 3m preference shares at Pounds 1 each.
The shares will carry warrants to subscribe for 520,231 ordinary Banner
shares at 173p each between October 22 1995 and July 31 2001 against a
mid-market share price on September 23 of 142p.
The proceeds will be used to increase Banner's land buying and housebuilding
activities.
