The Hoare Govett Smaller Companies index (capital gains version) was
virtually unchanged at 1824.12 in the week to 10 March. The FT-SE-A
All-Share index fell 0.3 per cent over the same period. Since the start of
this year the HGSC index has risen 7.9 per cent, while the All-Share has
fallen 2.8 per cent.
