The New Throgmorton Trust (1983), a split capital trust, saw net asset value
per capital share, taking prior charges at par, dive to 99.5p as at
September 30.
The figure compared with 128.2p at the March year-end and 188.8p at the
interim stage of 1991.
Net revenue for the six months to end-September fell to Pounds 734,000
(Pounds 1.75m). Earnings per share declined to 1.88p (4.48p) but the second
interim dividend is maintained at 1.5p.
