Cost controls, together with a reduction in interest charges, enabled TDS
Circuits, the USM-traded printed circuit board maker, to reduce losses from
Pounds 488,000 to Pounds 196,000 pre-tax for the six months to August 31.
Turnover totalled Pounds 3.4m (Pounds 3.5m) and losses per share emerged at
3.84p (7.08p).
