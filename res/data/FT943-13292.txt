Net asset value per share of Trust of Property Shares rose 7 per cent from
82.43p to 88.18p, against a fall of 17 per cent in the FT-SE-A Property
Index, for the six months to June 30.
Available revenue more than doubled from Pounds 30,518 to Pounds 64,792.
Earnings per share increased from 0.46p to 0.98p.
