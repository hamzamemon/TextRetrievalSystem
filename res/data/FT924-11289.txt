ARGENTINA'S economy minister, Mr Domingo Cavallo, flies to Brazil tomorrow
to discuss ways of overcoming his country's widening trade deficit with
Brazil, John Barham reports from Buenos Aires. Mr Cavallo, stopping over on
his way to Europe, will meet Brazil's finance, planning and trade and
industry ministers. Brazil, Argentina's largest trading partner, had a
Dollars 900m surplus with Argentina in the first eight months of the year.
