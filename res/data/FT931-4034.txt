Eidos, the USM-quoted video editing systems company, cut its 1992 pre-tax
loss from Pounds 385,000 to Pounds 190,000. Turnover advanced to Pounds
110,000, against Pounds 3,200.
The company's net asset value has fallen to less than half the nominal value
of its shares and an extraordinary meeting has been called.
To provide additional working capital Lancaster Participations is making a
Pounds 50,000 facility available secured on certain assets.
Losses per share were 8.66p (17.5p).
